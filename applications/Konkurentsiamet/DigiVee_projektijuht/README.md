## Project Context: DigiVee

DigiVee is a national cooperation platform being developed alongside water reform to support:

- Local governments and water companies in strategic management
- Resource usage optimization
- Transparent reporting and compliance
- Digital transformation of Estonia's water management sector

The project manager coordinates Competition Authority's role in ensuring digital solutions meet both state and regulatory requirements.
