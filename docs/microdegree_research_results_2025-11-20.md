### Task 2.1: Credential Validation (for Free Options)

#### **1. Google Project Management Certificate (via Free Trial)**

- **Recognition in Estonia:**
  - While not as universally recognized as a PMP, it provides foundational skills valued in entry-level to mid-level PM roles.
  - It can serve as a strong stepping stone and demonstrate initiative.
  - LinkedIn search for Estonian professionals with this credential might yield results, but its direct impact on job postings is less explicit than PMP.
- **ROI Analysis:**
  - **Average time to complete:** 3-6 months (but can be accelerated for free trial).
  - **Total cost:** **Free** (if completed within the 7-day trial for at least the first course).
  - **Salary increase potential in Estonia:** Unlikely to directly lead to a significant salary increase on its own, but enhances employability for entry/mid-level roles.
  - **Job placement rate:** Google reports 75% positive career outcomes globally, but specific data for Estonia is unavailable.
- **Community & Networking:**
  - Coursera platform offers some community features.
  - Google's employer consortium is primarily US-focused, so direct networking benefits in Estonia are limited.

#### **2. PMI Kickoff**

- **Recognition in Estonia:**
  - Provides a digital badge from the Project Management Institute (PMI), a globally recognized authority.
  - Valuable for demonstrating basic understanding and commitment to PM.
- **ROI Analysis:**
  - **Average time to complete:** 45 minutes âš¡

  - **Salary increase potential in Estonia:** Minimal direct impact, but a good resume builder for foundational knowledge.

#### **3. Scrum.org Free Learning Paths**

### Task 2.2: Application Pathway (for Google Project Management Certificate)

#### **Output: Action Plan Document with Timeline**

**Goal:** Obtain verifiable credentials and foundational project management knowledge at no cost to enhance job search prospects in Estonia.

**Phase 1: Immediate Action (Next 1-2 Weeks)**

- **Day 1-2: Complete PMI Kickoff**
  - **Action:** Enroll and complete the free [PMI Kickoff](https://www.pmi.org/kickoff) course.
  - **Outcome:** Earn a digital badge, gain basic PM vocabulary and concepts.
  - **CV/LinkedIn:** Add "PMI Kickoff Digital Badge" to your LinkedIn profile and CV under "Certifications" or "Professional Development."
- **Day 3-7: Google Project Management Certificate - Free Trial Sprint**
  - **Action:** Sign up for the 7-day free trial of the [Google Project Management Professional Certificate](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-project-management) on Coursera.
  - **Focus:** Dedicate significant time (e.g., 5-8 hours/day) to complete **Course 1: Foundations of Project Management**. Aim to finish as much as possible within the trial.
  - **Outcome:** If Course 1 is completed within the trial, you will receive a verifiable certificate for that course. Even if not fully completed, you will gain substantial knowledge.
  - **CV/LinkedIn:** If Course 1 is completed, list it as "Google Project Management Professional Certificate (Course 1: Foundations of Project Management - Completed, [Date])" on your CV and LinkedIn. If not, you can still mention "Actively studying Google Project Management Professional Certificate (Coursera)."
- **Ongoing: Scrum.org Learning Paths**
  - **Action:** Explore the free [Scrum.org Learning Paths](https://www.scrum.org/learning-paths) for Product Owner and Agile Leader.
  - **Focus:** Read through the materials to build a strong understanding of Agile and Scrum principles.
  - **Outcome:** Deepen your knowledge of Agile methodologies, which are highly sought after.
  - **CV/LinkedIn:** Integrate Agile/Scrum keywords and concepts into your experience descriptions.

**Phase 2: Continuous Learning & Application (Ongoing)**

- **Apply Knowledge:** Actively look for opportunities to apply PM and Agile concepts in your daily life or volunteer work.
- **Interview Preparation:** Be ready to discuss what you've learned from these free resources in job interviews, emphasizing practical application.
- **Networking:** Look for local Estonian project management or Agile meetups (online or in-person) to connect with professionals.
